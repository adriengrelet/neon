TITLE: Hacker groups and networks

GhostMesh:
Decentralized relays, favors low-signature operations.
Strength: discipline under pressure.
Weakness: trust builds slowly, sometimes too slowly.

Roofline Collective (ZE08):
Neighborhood mutual-aid technicians.
Strength: local legitimacy and practical repairs.
Weakness: exposed infrastructure and burnout.

Free Kernel Assembly (DX90 lineage):
Open-source radicals maintaining covert toolchains.
Strength: resilient code commons.
Weakness: endless internal debates, uneven op readiness.

Night Couriers:
Data runners moving files physically when networks are hostile.
Strength: hard to trace at scale.
Weakness: high personal risk and little legal cover.
