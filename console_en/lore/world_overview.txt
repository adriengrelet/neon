TITLE: Neon Node world overview

Cities are stacked like debt ledgers: premium air on top, heat and mold below.
Most services are privatized, then sold back at variable rates.
People do not disappear all at once; they are removed from systems one permission at a time.

The megastructure is not a building, it is governance software wearing concrete.
Inside it, law is implemented as latency, checkpoints, and score thresholds.
Outside it, communities improvise survival with mutual aid, hacked utilities, and stolen bandwidth.
