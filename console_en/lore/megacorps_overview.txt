TITLE: Megacorporate landscape

1) Kheiron Dynamics
Owns security stacks, Sentinel models, and compliance linguistics.
Public slogan: Safe systems, safe society.
Internal KPI: incident containment per credit.

2) Helix Habitat
Controls modular housing, eviction automation, and water access contracts.
Their app says homes are flexible. Tenants call it rotating precarity.

3) Vanta Biologics
Runs clinic subscriptions and biometric debt scoring.
Miss one payment and your treatment priority quietly drops.

4) Straylight Transit
Operates metro AI and route permissions.
Known issue: ghost lines where old maps remember people the system no longer serves.
