TITLE: Infrastructure notes

Power:
Official grid is dynamic-priced by district risk score.
Blackouts are called balancing events.
Hospitals in low-score zones get delayed restoration.

Housing:
Leases are algorithmic. Appeals are mostly performative.
One typo in an identity field can trigger automated displacement.

Network:
Public net has strict packet shaping.
Underground mesh links bridge dead zones with rooftop repeaters and stolen antennas.

Food and water:
Distribution priority follows labor score and debt compliance.
Communes offset shortages with kitchens, repair swaps, and gray-market logistics.
