MISSION 004 - Data heist for beginners

Goal:
Extract one policy memo proving harm behind corporate language.

Steps:
1. Prioritize files with legal or compliance headers.
2. Copy evidence first, side loot second.
3. Store findings in archive/ with date and source node.
4. Send summary to allies, not the whole raw dump.

Why this matters:
A single signed memo can move a district faster than 100 rumors.
