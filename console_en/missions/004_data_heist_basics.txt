MISSION 004 - Data heist for beginners

Intent:
Stealing data matters only if the proof survives the theft, the panic, and the speech that will later try to strip it of meaning.

Objective:
Extract a policy memo or compliance fragment capable of proving the violence hidden behind corporate language.

Steps:
1. Prioritize legal/, compliance/, and signed validation traces. Sleek interfaces lie better than appendices.
2. Copy the proof first. Side loot comes later, if you are still breathing calmly enough to earn it.
3. Store findings in archive/ with date, source node, and minimal context. Proof without provenance quickly becomes a well-packaged rumor.
4. Send allies a summary, not the raw dump. Truth travels better when it does not crush the people who need to use it.

Why it matters:
A single signed memo can move a district faster than a hundred rumors, because it forces the official lie to speak in its own voice.
