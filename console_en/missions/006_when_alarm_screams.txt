MISSION 006 - When alarm screams

Goal:
Survive an alarm spike and still complete extraction.

Steps:
1. Accept that plan A is gone. Plan B exists for a reason.
2. Prioritize mobility over hero moments.
3. If Sentinel appears, disengage unless mission-critical.
4. Spend energy on actions that create distance.

Emergency humor:
If the walls feel loud, congratulations, the building knows your name.
