MISSION 006 - When the alarm screams

Intent:
The moment the alarm climbs is not the moment to become poetic. It is the moment to accept that the original plan is dead and that your only useful elegance is still called exit.

Objective:
Survive an alarm spike, preserve what matters, and still finish extraction without turning yourself into a posthumous heroic gesture.

Steps:
1. Accept immediately that plan A is dead. Plan B exists for exactly this minute.
2. Prioritize mobility, reading escape lines, and distance. Heroic moments are expensive and mostly useful to obituaries.
3. If a Sentinel appears, disengage unless the critical mission truly forces you to hold position.
4. Spend energy on actions that open, move, or blur. Any expense that creates no margin should be treated with suspicion.

Emergency humor:
If the walls sound loud, congratulations: the building already knows your name, your rhythm, and probably your direction.
