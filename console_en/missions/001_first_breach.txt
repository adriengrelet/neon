MISSION 001 - First breach without drama

Intent:
The first run is not there to impress anyone. It is there to bring you back with your fingers, your breath, and enough calm to do it again tomorrow.

Objective:
Get in, recover a file with no major strategic value, get out alive, and leave behind an impression of banality.

Steps:
1. Run a scan before any advance. Curiosity almost always costs less than panic.
2. Watch the alarm like a rent meter: ignore it too long and your life becomes the variable.
3. Hack a single terminal. Beginners die of greed more often than of lack of talent.
4. Exfil as soon as the objective is validated. A clean exit is worth more than one extra room.

Field reminder:
If you catch yourself saying "just one more room," understand that the tutorial for danger has already begun without telling you.
