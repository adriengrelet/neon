MISSION 001 - First breach without drama

Goal:
Enter, grab one harmless file, leave alive, look unimpressive.

Steps:
1. Run scan first. Curiosity is cheaper than panic.
2. Watch alarm level like it is your rent meter.
3. Hack one terminal only. Beginners die from greed, not from lack of skill.
4. Exfil as soon as objective is done.

Beginner joke that is not really a joke:
If you say "just one more room," you are already in the danger tutorial.
