MISSION 003 - Terminal dance basics

Goal:
Practice matrix hacks under time pressure without tunnel vision.

Steps:
1. Read sequence twice before typing once.
2. Step 2 follows column, step 3 follows row.
3. If your pulse spikes, breathe, then input.
4. Fast clean hack > flashy risky hack.

Humor from veterans:
Typing random coordinates is cardio, not strategy.
