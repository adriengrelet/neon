MISSION 003 - Basics of terminal dance

Intent:
Matrix hacking is not a contest of raw speed. It is a discipline of reading, rhythm, and composure under a hostile timer.

Objective:
Practice timed hack sequences without collapsing into tunnel vision or turning your own fingers into an error source.

Steps:
1. Read the sequence twice before entering a single coordinate.
2. Remember the rule: step 2 follows the column, step 3 follows the row. The system loves punishing people who know the rule and rush anyway.
3. If your pulse spikes, breathe, recenter, then validate. One recovered second is better than an immediate failure.
4. A clean fast hack beats a flashy embarrassing one.

Veteran humor:
Typing coordinates at random is not instinctive talent.
It is just cardio with consequences.
