MISSION 002 - Open, mask, leave

Intent:
A good intrusion does not only open a door. It leaves behind a lie credible enough to delay the real question: who came through here?

Objective:
Open a locked path, cross it cleanly, and make it look as if maintenance, negligence, or bad firmware did the work for you.

Steps:
1. Use echo before pushing into an unknown zone. Walls speak sooner than guards.
2. Hack the lock node, then reduce the alarm if the window still allows it.
3. Drop a fake maintenance beacon into logs/ if you still have time and steady hands.
4. Never fight a Sentinel for style. Style does not survive incident reports.

Reminder:
Stealth is not invisibility.
Stealth is giving the system a story plausible enough that it wastes time lying to itself.
