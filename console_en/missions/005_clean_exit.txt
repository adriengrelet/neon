MISSION 005 - Clean exfil discipline

Intent:
A run does not end when you reach the core. It ends when you get out with the proof, your breath, and a reasonable chance of still being free tomorrow.

Objective:
Leave behind as few easy traces as possible, protect your future self, and get out before fatigue starts selling you out.

Steps:
1. Check inventory, HP, and energy before the last corridor. Many deaths begin with a calculation postponed for later.
2. Avoid optional fights near extraction. Nothing is more ridiculous than a tactical victory that cancels a guaranteed exit.
3. Confirm the objective is complete, then leave immediately. The building does not need your pride as an optional add-on.
4. Write a short debrief while memory is still fresh. A good field note saves you from repeating the same mistake with nobler words.

Hallway proverb:
The best exfil is the one nobody notices tomorrow and the one you can still tell the day after.
